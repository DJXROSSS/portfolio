UI/UX Web Design 2023

E-Commerce Platform Redesign

A complete redesign of a modern e-commerce platform focused on improving conversion rates and enhancing the overall user journey through intuitive design patterns and seamless interactions.

Project Overview

This project involved a comprehensive redesign of an existing e-commerce platform that was experiencing declining conversion rates and high cart abandonment. The goal was to create a modern, user-friendly shopping experience that would increase sales and improve customer satisfaction.

Through extensive user research and competitive analysis, we identified key pain points in the existing design and developed solutions that addressed both business objectives and user needs.

The Challenge

The existing platform had several critical issues:

1

High Cart Abandonment

72% of users were abandoning their carts before completing purchase

2

Poor Mobile Experience

Mobile users reported difficulty navigating and completing purchases

3

Outdated Visual Design

The interface felt dated and didn't reflect the brand's modern positioning

The Solution

We developed a comprehensive redesign strategy focusing on:

Streamlined checkout process reducing steps from 6 to 3

Mobile-first responsive design with touch-optimized interactions

Modern visual language with improved typography and spacing

Enhanced product discovery with smart filtering and recommendations

Improved accessibility meeting WCAG 2.1 AA standards
